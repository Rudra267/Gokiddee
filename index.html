About Us

Welcome to Gokiddee, a platform where we make sustainability practical and accessible in the world of kids' products. Our founder Revathi Gollapudi’s mission is clear and impactful: to minimize waste, protect the environment, and empower parents to make eco-friendly choices for their children.At Gokiddee, we believe that every little step counts in creating a better future for our kids. That's why we've embraced the 4 R's—Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, and Rent—as the cornerstone of our approach.

  • Reduce Waste : We encourage the purchase of eco-friendly products designed with the environment in mind. By choosing items made from sustainable materials and produced with minimal environmental impact, you can help reduce the waste generated by children's products.
