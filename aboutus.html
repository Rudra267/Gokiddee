About Us

Welcome to Gokiddee, where sustainability meets practicality in the world of kids' products. Founded by Revathi Gollapudi, Gokiddee was born out of a deep-rooted desire to create a greener, more sustainable future for children while making eco-friendly choices accessible to parents. At Gokiddee, we believe that the small, thoughtful choices we make today will lead to a better tomorrow—for our kids, their future, and our planet.

Our commitment is simple yet powerful: reduce waste, protect the environment, and empower families to embrace eco-conscious living. We are on a mission to make sustainability easy, affordable, and practical for parents, by offering products and services that prioritize the well-being of both your child and the Earth.

Our Vision

At Gokiddee, our vision is to lead a movement where sustainability is not a trend but a way of life. We aspire to build a future where eco-friendly choices are the norm in parenting and children's products, creating a cleaner, greener world for future generations. By providing parents with accessible, sustainable options, we envision a world where every child grows up with a profound respect for the planet and its resources.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make sustainability practical , simple, and affordable for parents. We aim to reduce waste, conserve resources, and foster eco-friendly living by offering an easy-to-use platform for purchasing, renting, reusing, and recycling children's products. Through innovation and education, we empower families to make informed decisions that benefit both their children and the Earth. Our goal is to change the way people think about kids' products, promoting a circular economy where resources are used more efficiently, and waste is minimized.

Our Core Values

  • Reduce Waste : We’re dedicated to helping families reduce waste by offering high-quality, eco-friendly products that are designed to have a minimal environmental impact. By choosing sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing processes, we encourage conscious consumption, helping to keep more waste out of landfills.

  • Reuse Preloved Items : Kids grow fast, and their needs change even faster! At Gokiddee, we provide a platform for parents to buy and sell preloved children's items, giving these products a second life. By reusing items, we reduce the demand for new resources, cut down on overproduction, and save families money while also helping the planet.

  • Recycle Responsibly : We promote responsible recycling practices by ensuring that products that have reached the end of their lifecycle are disposed of in an environmentally-friendly way. Moreover, we encourage upcycling, helping parents transform old products into something new and useful, keeping waste out of landfills.

  • Rent for Short-Term Needs : Why buy something you'll only use for a few months? Gokiddee offers a rental service for short-term items like strollers, car seats, baby carriers, or special occasion outfits. Renting not only saves you money but also reduces the environmental impact by lowering the need for mass production and excess consumption.

Our Sustainability Goals

➟ We are committed to setting measurable goals that help make a real impact on the environment. Our sustainability objectives include :

  • Minimizing Waste

    By offering sustainable products and supporting the resale of preloved items, we aim to keep a significant amount of waste out of landfills.

  • Reducing Carbon Footprint

    Through the use of eco-friendly shipping methods, local sourcing, and promoting the sharing economy, we reduce the carbon footprint of our operations and products.

  • Supporting Circular Economy

    By encouraging the reuse and rental of products, we contribute to a circular economy where resources are kept in use for as long as possible before they are recycled into new products.

How We Make a Difference

Empowering Parents to Make Better Choices

At Gokiddee, we provide parents with eco-friendly alternatives to conventional kids' products. Our curated selection includes items made from sustainable materials, ethically sourced, and designed to last. With every purchase, rental, or resale, parents can feel confident that they are making a positive contribution to a greener future.

Bridging the Gap Between Convenience and Sustainability

Sustainability shouldn't feel like a burden. We've made it easy for parents to adopt greener habits, whether it's through renting a product they only need temporarily, buying preloved items at a fraction of the cost, or recycling old goods responsibly. Our platform streamlines the process of making sustainable choices, allowing families to contribute to the planet's well-being without sacrificing convenience or quality.

Creating a Ripple Effect for Future Generations

Every product that gets reused, rented, or recycled is a step toward a more sustainable lifestyle. By supporting Gokiddee, parents are not only making a direct impact on their child’s future but also fostering a cultural shift where sustainability is ingrained in the way we raise the next generation.

Educational Initiatives

We believe in the power of knowledge and advocacy. Through our platform, blog, and outreach programs, we educate families about the importance of sustainability in children’s products. Our content aims to inspire parents to rethink traditional consumer habits and embrace a more circular, eco-conscious way of life.

Join Us in Creating a Sustainable Future

Gokiddee is more than just a marketplace—it's a movement. A movement towards a future where our kids can grow up in a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable world. We are committed to building a community of parents, families, and organizations that prioritize sustainability in all aspects of life. Together, we can make a measurable difference, one eco-friendly choice at a time.

Let’s take action now to protect our children’s future, reduce waste, conserve precious resources, and create a brighter, greener world for generations to come.

Thank you for being a part of our journey toward a more sustainable future. Together, we can make a meaningful impact on the planet, one small step at a time.
